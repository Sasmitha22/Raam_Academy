    About Us

    • Found in 2024

    Raam Academy is a young team dedicated to guide students in transforming their passions into purposeful careers. We help students to navigate this process by making them explore the principles of IKIGAI and guide them to make their personalised IKIGAI methods. This helps them to gain a better understanding of themselves. Our one-to-one mentorship gives students insights on how to progress their action plan. Through engaging storytelling and interactive discussions we conduct talk sessions and workshops that are both informative and enjoyable, capturing the attention of students. Together, we are fostering a generation of individuals ready to embrace their passions as professions.
